Carbon Tax in Canada

Background

Scientists have been well aware of global warming for several years. A United Nations Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change report in 2007 warned that artificial greenhouse gas emissions are the likely cause of global warming, which was supported by over 2000 climate scientists, including Canadians. Climate scientists predict that Canada will be especially impacted due to its cold weather. Canada has 9% of the global renewable water supply, with nearly 900,000 square kilometres of water.
